About Me

Who I Am

Hey there! I’m a Sales Engineer and Demo Systems Specialist with over 15 years of experience across pre-sales and post-sales roles. I’ve always been drawn to technology. Not just how it works, but how it can be used to tell better stories, drive clarity, and create impact across go-to-market teams.

My focus these days is on building scalable demo environments, internal tooling, and AI-powered bots that help Sales, Product, and Enablement teams show up stronger. I love taking complex platforms and turning them into intuitive, high-impact stories that resonate with real buyers.

When I’m not working, I’m probably laughing with my three daughters, chasing their curiosity, or tinkering with AI tools and workflows just to see what’s possible. That same curiosity fuels my work, whether it’s testing a new feature, building a custom demo flow, or figuring out how to connect the dots across a product launch.

What drives me most is the belief that technical clarity leads to sales confidence. If I can help a rep walk into a meeting feeling prepared, confident, and clear on the “why” behind what they’re showing.. that's a win.

About Me

Who I Am

Hey there! I’m a Sales Engineer with 15 years of experience in both pre-sales and post-sales roles. I’ve always been drawn to technology—not just how it works, but how it can be used to solve real problems. I love exploring new tools, tinkering with ideas, and finding creative ways to build better solutions. That curiosity is what keeps me excited every day.

When I’m not working, my world revolves around my three little girls. They keep me on my toes, constantly learning, and always laughing. And when I do get a little time to myself, you’ll probably find me diving into AI—experimenting, building, and seeing what’s possible.

One thing I truly believe in is the power of knowledge sharing. My goal is to make sure every sales rep I work with walks into their meetings feeling confident and well-prepared from the technical side of the conversation. Watching them succeed is just as fulfilling as any win of my own.

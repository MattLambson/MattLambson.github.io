000 - Welcome to Field Operations

I built this site for a pretty simple reason. I wanted to see if I could.

Most of my day is spent at the intersection of product, engineering, and sales. I’m usually the person tasked with explaining how complex systems actually work—and more importantly, why they sometimes don’t. These notes are an extension of that "under the hood" reality.

Think of this section as a digital scratchpad. It’s where I’ll be dropping:

  • Architectural Stress Tests: Breaking down why certain "elegant" solutions are actually fragile in production.
  • Agentic AI Realities: Moving past the hype to look at state management, handoffs, and where the plumbing usually leaks.
  • Field Observations: Lessons learned from the front lines of CPaaS and CX.
  • The Occasional Tangent: Expect some inner monologues, the odd technical deep-dive, or just a record of a specific problem I had to solve that week.

I’m not interested in curated corporate hype. My goal is to build a body of work that speaks for itself. If these notes happen to be useful to anyone else, that’s a win.

I don’t have a comment section here because I’d rather spend my time building than moderating. If you want to discuss a post or tell me my logic is flawed, find me over on LinkedIn. That’s where the conversation happens.

Everything here is just the raw data. Welcome to the lab.
